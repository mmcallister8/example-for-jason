About us

Why we exist

Built after watching good clinicians get burned by bad ads

We started in 2023 after running marketing inside a cash practice and watching three generic agencies burn its budget on leads that never showed. The playbook that finally worked, cash-pay qualification up front, self-scheduling, and follow-up within 5 minutes, became this agency.

Since then we've run campaigns for 31 practices and booked more than 2,400 evaluations. We stay small on purpose: 4 new clients a month, one practice per market, so we never compete against you with your own playbook.
